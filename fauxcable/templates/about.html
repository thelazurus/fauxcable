About FauxCable

Self-hosted EPG enrichment with poster art.

View on GitHub

FauxCable was built entirely through conversational AI. It reads an XMLTV feed, enriches it with poster art from TVMaze and TMDB, and serves the result at /epg.xml for Jellyfin, Emby, and similar media servers.
